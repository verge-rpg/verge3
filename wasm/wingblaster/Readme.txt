Wing Blaster
============

Story
-----
Your planet was destroyed. You were on vacation at the time. Now it's time for revenge. You steal a ship at the tropical resort and launch yourself into space. "I will avenge you!", you exclaim as your vehicle is propelled forward at an alarming speed.

Gameplay
--------
Travel through space, dodge large hails of bullets, and kill the enemy. If you beat the game, announce your score and death count, I might be amazed!

Controls
--------
Arrows	Move
Space	Fire
